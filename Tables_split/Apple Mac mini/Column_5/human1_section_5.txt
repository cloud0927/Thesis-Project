Overall, the users are satisfied with the product for daily use, mentioning high speed especially with M4 chips. However, they reported that its storage and RAM are not enough for heavy work, making it slow. It makes users buy external drives and GPUs. Therefore, it is suggested to set the target customers- daily use or heavy work. Develop clear advertisements and guidance to customers, or enhance its storage.
